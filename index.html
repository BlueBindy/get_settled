Welcome to Get Settled, Ireland

If you are a Brit looking to understand how to apply for Irish citizenship, we'd love to help. This website is the research collected by a small group of us who are either in the process of applying, about to apply or have received citizenship.

In all honestly, we found the process confusing at times. We wanted to bring the information that made it easier into one place and only include information from official sources.
